CREATIVE BRIEF

Faux-scent called UMBRA. It's a dark & cold scent—ideal for only the most exclusive
black-tie events—a poetic touch to a stern, enigmatic character. This launch would
include a new label design for the bottle (consider it a special edition) and of course,
a magazine ad spread.

COLOGNE DESIGN

Thinking of the definition of umbra, I was thinking how I could play with light and
shadow to abstractly interpret the meaning itself. Using the copyright Hawthorne provided
me, "Catch your own shadow' not too opaque, just a glimpse of it, quite mysterious.
That line was originally for the magazine spread, but I decided to include it in the
cologne design because I felt like it captured what the faux-scent umbra was all about.

MAGAZINE AD

The design intent here was to create a magazine spread to exude the dark, scent with the
poetic touch. I felt a monochromatic palette was only right with tonal range. The bottle
was quite minimalistic, so I wanted the magazine spread to add delight by adding flowers
daintily framing the cologne. There's a lot of white negative space to balance the
darkness of the cologne, but also not to take away its shine.

Copyright was provided by Hawthrone.
